Rare Books & Special Collections

Our Special Collections houses over 100,000 rare books and manuscripts, including a Gutenberg Bible leaf and one of the finest collections of Texana in the world. Materials are available for research by appointment.

Schedule a visit

Special Collections is open to all researchers by appointment. Contact our reading room staff to schedule a visit and discuss your research needs in advance.

Researchers may use personal cameras or phones to photograph non-restricted materials at no charge. High-resolution scanning services are also available through our digital lab.

Many collections have been digitized and are available through our digital repository. Search our finding aids to discover what's available before your visit.

Our collections are open to any researcher — TAMU students, faculty, and the general public alike.

Need research help?

Our subject librarians are available for one-on-one consultations to help you find the right sources for your project.
